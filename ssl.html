The Surviving Student Life (SSL) web app is a dynamic service aimed at answering the more common questions that arise during induction and during your time at University in general. From letting you know your halls address to how to find information about MSU clubs and societies, Surviving Student Life is filled with essential info.

It also features some fantastic advice and information for maintaining your well-being during your time at University, focusing on the academic, emotional, financial, spiritual and social aspects of your life.
