Marjon Induction - marjon Mobile App

Marjon Mobile is a web app designed to compliment your life at Marjon. It has been designed from the ground up as a mobile friendly feature rich application that gives you access to a range of personalised information and campus updates. Features include:

  • Colour coded 2-week timetable that links locations to the interactive campus map
  • Interactive campus map with room search functionality
  • Live news feeds pulled directly from the HUB and LearningSpace and event listings
  • A list of your current Library loans
  • 3-day campus weather forecast
  • Live departure and route information for all bus services running from campus to city centre
  • Comprehensive staff contact listings

You will be able to access Marjon Mobile in the week following your registration as you will be required to log in using your student IT account details. This means that the app can provide you with personalised information such as your timetable or current Library loans.
