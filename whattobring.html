Checklist

Be prepared!

This checklist is designed to make sure you arrive on campus with everything you need. In the months leading up to induction, you can check items off of your list by simply pressing the item in question. Your selections will be remembered for the next time you visit the page;

More specific details about items in the checklist can be found by pressing the question mark icon to the right of the item.

This checklist offers you a comprehensive list of what you need to have with you when you arrive at Marjon. Check it often and make sure nothing is left at home!

The following three items will be required during the enrolment process and it is vital that you make sure you bring them with you:

Enrolment:

Help

For photo ID we will accept any current and valid Passport, a UK Biometric Residence Permit, a current UK/Isle of Man/Channel Islands Driving License card with counterpart paper license, or a UK/Channel Islands Birth Certificate (including those issued by UK authorities overseas e.g. Embassies, High Commisions and HM Forces).

Help

Your welcome letter will be scanned during enrolment in order to generate your picture on screen which will then be checked with your photo ID.

Help

This will be the letter you recieved from Student Finance containing a barcode which may be scanned by the Accounts department if required.
