Fresher's Week

The MSU are delighted to announce the full programme of events for Fresher's Week 2013/2014. Prices for entry, times and further acts will be released via the MSU website and Facebook page in due course. All events are at Marjon unless stated otherwise.

  • Events Schedule*

  • Monday 16th Sept - Welcome Night
  • Tuesday 17th Sept - Back to School
  • Wednesday 18th Sept - Paint Party (Oceana)
  • Thursday 19th Sept - Bar Crawl & Booze Cruise (End Party at H20 Barbican)
  • Friday 20th Sept - Halls Night
  • Saturday 21st Sept - Acoustic Night
  • Sunday 22nd Sept - Hypnotist Night
  • Monday 23rd Sept - Bierkeller (Walkabout)
  • Tuesday 24th Sept - Deal or No Deal
  • Wednesday 25th Sept - Lads & Ladies Night Ft Lethal Bizzle (Oceana)
  • Thursday 26th Sept - Film Night
  • Friday 27th Sept - Toga Night
  • Saturday 28th Sept - Inflatable Olympics
  • Sunday 29th Sept - Moff & Jack's Big Quiz
