Fresher's Week

Your Marjon Student Union (MSU) officers will be working feverishly over the summer to make sure your Fresher's Week is an unforgettable one. The full line-up of events will be completed a little closer to September so make sure you check the MSU website regularly for all of the latest details:

Marjon student Union Website

In the mean time, here's a quick introduction to your MSU officers for next year:

Ryan Ellis & Casey Candler

MSU Title's: Joint Diversity Officers

About Us: Hello freshers, we are your joint diversity officers, this is a quick message to introduce ourselves. We are both studying in Education and have been elected to support you as students this year. We have a few plans this year and if you ever want to get into contact with us feel free to message either of us on Facebook. We look forward to meeting you all and enjoy freshers! :)

Victoria Lee

Nickname: Vix

MSU Title: Mature Student Officer

About Me: This is my 3rd year at Marjon and having been a Student Rep for the last two years, I wanted to go that little bit further when it comes to representing my fellow students. As we make up such a large part of the student body, it's important that the Mature Students of Marjon have a voice. By working with the other Student Union officers, I hope to help make the experience of coming to study at Marjon, as rewarding and fun as possible.

Jack Levick

MSU Title: Deputy President

About Me: For the last 3 years I have been studying media production and have been heavily involved in the student union, being a member of the marjon union social team as well as being elected as Entertaiment's Officer as well as a Senior Resident Student (SRS) in my third year. This year I will be your Deputy President and I look forward to meeting you all.
